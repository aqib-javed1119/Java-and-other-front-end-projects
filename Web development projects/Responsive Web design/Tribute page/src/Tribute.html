Salman Khan

Bollywood Actor

Salman Khan is a very popular Bollywood actor in India

Salman Khan is an Indian film actor, producer, occasional singer and television personality who works in Hindi films. In a film career spanning over thirty years, Khan has received numerous awards, including two National Film Awards as a film producer, and two Filmfare Awards for acting.

He is cited in the media as one of the most commercially successful actors of both world and Indian cinema. Forbes included him in their 2015 list of Top-Paid 100 Celebrity Entertainers in the world; Khan tied with Actor Amitabh Bachchan for No. 71 on the list, both with earnings of $33.5 million.According to the Forbes 2018 list of Top-Paid 100 Celebrity Entertainers in world, Khan was the highest-ranked Indian with 82nd rank with earnings of $37.7 million.He is also known as the host of the reality show, Bigg Boss since 2010

Salman Khan began his acting career with a supporting role in movie "Biwi Ho To Aisi"(1988), followed by a leading role in movie "Maine Pyar Kiya "(1989). Salman Khan continued in Bollywood in the 1990s with roles in several productions, including the romantic drama "Hum Aapke Hain Koun..!" (1994), the action thriller movie "Karan Arjun"(1995), the comedy movie"Biwi No.1"(1999), and the family drama "Hum Saath-Saath Hain" (1999).

After a brief period of decline in the 2000s, Salman Khan achieved greater stardom in the 2010s by playing the lead role in successful action movies like "Dabangg" (2010), "Ready" (2011), "Ek Tha Tiger" (2012), "Kick" (2014), "Sultan" (2016) and "Tiger Zinda Hai" . (2017) .
